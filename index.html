Starting out

When starting your job search, it's crucial to identify your interests and understand which roles align with your skills. Different companies offer a variety of positions, each with its own level of competitiveness. Begin by focusing on a broad area of interest, which will help guide you toward more specific roles offered by individual companies. Identifying a general field of interest is the first step in narrowing down the specific opportunities that best fit your background and aspirations.

Companies

When applying for positions, it's essential to be familiar with key players in the banking industry. In this section, you'll find some of the most prominent and prestigious companies in the field, known for offering top-tier salaries. However, these firms are highly competitive, as many people aspire to join them. Conducting thorough company research is crucial when applying, and this should serve as your starting point in that process.

JP Morgan

JPMorgan Chase & Co. is an American multinational finance company headquartered in New York City and incorporated in Delaware. It is the largest bank in the United States and the world's largest bank by market capitalization as of 2023.
